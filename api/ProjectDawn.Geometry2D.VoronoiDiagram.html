    Struct VoronoiDiagram

    Voronoi diagram composed of cells, edges and vertices. Voronoi diagram is a partition of a plane into regions close to each of a given set of objects. In the simplest case, these objects are just finitely many points in the plane (called seeds, sites, or generators). For each seed there is a corresponding region, called a Voronoi cell, consisting of all points of the plane closer to that seed than to any other. Use Construct<T>(ref T) to construct this voronoi diagram.
